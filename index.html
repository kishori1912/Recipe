Prawns Pulao

Prawn Pulao or Shrimp Pulao is a simple and flavourful recipe.It is so easy to make and quick, making it apt for your everyday meals or even to pack in your Office Lunch Box.

Timing and Serving

  • Course: Main
    Course Cuisine: Indian
  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
    Cook Time: 45 minutes
    Total Time: 1 hour
  • Servings: 4 people
    Calories: 424kcal
  • INGRADIENTS
    200grm prawns 250grm basmati rice 3 tbsp oil or ghee for green paste-4 green chilis,1 tomato chopped, 1 cup grated coconut fresh 1 cup coriander and mint leaves, ginger and garlic paste Whole spices- 3 green cardamom,7 peppercorn, 2 cinnamon sticks, 4 cloves, 2 bay leaves Spices powder-2 tbsp coriander powder, 1 tbsp red chili powder, 2 tbsp garam masala, 1/2 tbsp turmeric powder other ingredients-chopped onion, lemon juice, salt as per required.

    • Wash Rice

    Rice take it aside for soak 20 mints.

    Green Paste

    For green paste - add green chili, fresh coconut, coriander and mint leaves in grinder.

    Green Paste

    Grind all ingredients with some water 1/2 cup until fine green paste.

    Whole Spices

    Take heavy bottom pan and add ghee2 tbsp and oil 2 tbsp in it keep gas on medium flame for 10 min.

    Whole Spices

    Add green cardamom, peppercorn, cinnamon sticks, cloves, bayleaves, chopped-onion.Saute until onion become nice golden brown in color.

    Powder Spices

    Add red chili, garam masala, coriander powder.Add gingergarlic paste,tomato and grind green past in pan cook for 2-3 mint.

    Add Prawns

    Add prawns.

    Rice Add

    Add rice, 2 cup of water and salt as per require.

    Serve

    Wait for 20 min untile rice is cooked. Serve.
